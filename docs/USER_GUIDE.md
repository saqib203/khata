# User Guide

## Daily Workflow

1. Open Dashboard to review receivables, payables, pump status, and inventory alerts.
2. Search customer by name, mobile number, WhatsApp number, or CNIC.
3. Open the customer profile to review balance, purchased pumps, invoices, and payment history.
4. Create sales invoices when pumps are delivered or sold.
5. Record installment payments from the payment screen or customer profile.
6. Check inventory before production starts and record parts used during pump work.
7. Generate daily reports before closing.

## Khata Rules

- Customer sale increases customer balance.
- Customer payment decreases customer balance.
- Supplier purchase increases payable balance.
- Supplier payment decreases payable balance.
